Latin quotes!

A. Fac ut vivas.
Get a life.
B. Recedite, plebes! Gero rem imperialem.
Stand aside plebians! I am on imperial business.
C. Totum dependeat.
Let it all hang out.
D. Ventis secundis, tene cursum.
Go with the flow.
E. Mellita, domi adsum.
Honey, I'm home.
F. Utinam logica falsa tuam philosophiam totam suffodiant!
May faulty logic undermine your entire philosophy!
G. Re vera, potas bene.
Say, you sure are drinking a lot.
H. Nihil curo de ista tua stulta superstitione.
I'm not interested in your dopey religious cult.
I. Vacca foeda.
Stupid cow.
J. Estne volumen in toga, an solum tibi libet me videre?
Is that a scroll in your toga, or are you just happy to see me?
